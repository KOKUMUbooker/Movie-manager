## ** The Problem**

Planning movie nights with friends is frustrating:
- **Endless group chats** debating what to watch
- **Scheduling conflicts** across time zones and busy lives
- **Streaming service fragmentation** - "Who has what?"
- **No fair way to decide** - Someone always compromises
- **Lost discussions** - Great movie conversations disappear in chat history

## ** The Solution**

GroupWatch is a **decision-making platform** that helps friends plan perfect movie nights in minutes. No more 50-message debatesâ€”just smart coordination.

### **How It Works:**

1. **Create a Group** - Make a home for your movie nights with friends
2. **Suggest & Vote** - Add movie options, vote up/down, veto absolute no-gos
3. **Schedule** - Find times that work for everyone, check streaming availability
4. **Watch & Discuss** - Mark when you watch, rate together, continue the conversation

## ** What Makes GroupWatch Different**

- **Not another streaming service** - We coordinate what you already have
- **Not just another chat app** - Built specifically for movie night decisions
- **Smart voting system** - Upvote/downvote with veto protection for fair decisions
- **Streaming reality check** - Shows what's actually watchable by your group
- **Post-movie experience** - Rate together, discuss with spoiler tags, build watch history

## ** Key Features**

### **For Planning:**
-  Persistent groups for friend circles
-  Real-time voting with veto power
-  Smart scheduling across time zones
-  Streaming service compatibility check

### **For Watching:**
-  Simple watch tracking ("We're watching now!")
-  Post-movie discussion threads
-  Group ratings and reviews
-  Watch history and statistics

### **For Connecting:**
-  Easy invite system (no account needed to vote)
-  Group chat for planning
-  Notifications and reminders
-  Mobile-friendly design

## ** Technical Stack**

- **Frontend**: Svelte + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS
- **Backend**: ASP.NET Core + Entity Framework
- **Database**: PostgreSQL
- **Real-time**: SignalR
- **APIs**: TMDB (movie data), JustWatch (streaming availability)
